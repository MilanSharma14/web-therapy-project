Understanding different type of mental health problems

A perfect way to understand your condition

Anxiety

Anxiety is characterized by excessive worry, nervousness, and fear, often leading to physical symptoms like increased heart rate and restlessness.

Depression

Depression involves persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, fatigue, and feelings of hopelessness, significantly affecting daily functioning and well-being.

Addiction

Addiction is a chronic condition characterized by compulsive substance use or behavior despite harmful consequences, leading to physical and psychological dependence.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder involves extreme mood swings, including manic highs and depressive lows, impacting energy levels, behavior, and daily functioning.
